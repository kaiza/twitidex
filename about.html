About

This is an experimental app that downloads data from Twitter and summarises the referenced hashtags, users and URLs for the specified feed.

To get started, enter a search term and press the search button or press Enter. The feed for the search term is displayed along with a summary of the tags/users/URLs. Click a hashtag or user to search by the item.
